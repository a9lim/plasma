About this simulation

What is Plasma?

A WebGPU-native 2D resistive magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) simulator. The grid-Eulerian finite-volume scheme uses HLLD Riemann solvers, PPM reconstruction, RK3 SSP time integration, and constrained transport to keep ∇·B at machine roundoff. Default preset shows the Orszag-Tang vortex; switch to Harris current sheet to watch magnetic reconnection develop.
