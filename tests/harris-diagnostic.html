Runs the production Sim under the Harris preset (N=256 by default, γ=5/3, η=1e-3, outflow N/S, periodic E/W). Every sampleEvery steps it reads back U0/U1/Bx/By to the CPU and computes a row of diagnostics. Stops on the first non-finite cell or at the step cap. Logs everything to console.log as JSON for Playwright.

What this measures

Every sampleEvery steps the harness reads U0/U1/Bx/By to CPU. Per-cell it recovers primitive (ρ, v, p, B) using the same cons_to_prim_mhd recipe the engine uses (DENSITY_FLOOR = 1e-6, PRESSURE_FLOOR default 1e-6). It counts cells where the floors triggered (ρ ≤ 1.001 × floor, p ≤ 1.001 × floor) — those indicate the engine is masking real over-relaxation. It also counts non-finite cells and reports the first (i, j) where a NaN appears. Conservation drift is reported relative to the IC totals (∫ρ over the interior cells changes by less than 1e-7 per step on a healthy run).
