How to interpret the slope

Expected: slope ≈ 3 in L1 (limited by RK3 SSP). Slope ≈ 2 is evidence the PPM limiter is clipping smooth extrema and motivates the PPM4 (McCorquodale-Colella 2011) extremum-preserving upgrade. Anything else (≪ 2 or ≫ 3) suggests a numerical bug somewhere in the engine stack.
